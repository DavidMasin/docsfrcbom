---
title: Glossary
---
**BOM** — Bill of Materials; list of everything needed to build a subsystem/robot.  
**COTS** — Commercial Off‑The‑Shelf part (no machining required).  
**In‑House** — Part you manufacture (router/mill/lathe/laser/3D print).  
**Process** — A machining/production step; *Pre‑Process*, *Process 1*, *Process 2*.  
**Current Process** — The next step a part needs based on counts you enter.

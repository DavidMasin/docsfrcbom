# 👋 Welcome to FRCBOM

**FRCBOM** is an **Onshape-integrated Bill of Materials (BOM) and manufacturing workflow system** built for **FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) teams**.

It helps your team move seamlessly from **CAD → Manufacturing → Assembly** with real-time collaboration and clear progress tracking.

---

## 🚀 What is FRCBOM?
FRCBOM is a web platform that:
- 📑 Connects directly to your **Onshape BOM templates**
- 🏭 Tracks manufacturing processes (**Pre-Process, Process 1, Process 2**)
- 📊 Provides **System Dashboards** with live progress stats
- 🤝 Supports **real-time collaboration** across the team
- ⬇️ Lets you **download CAD** in the right format for your machines
- 🎉 Celebrates progress with confetti and visual 3D updates

---

## 🧩 How it Fits in Your Workflow
1. **In Onshape**
    - Create custom properties (`FRCBOM - Pre Process`, `FRCBOM - Process 1`, etc.)
    - Apply the **FRCBOM BOM template** to assemblies

2. **In FRCBOM**
    - Register your team and set up Admin/User accounts
    - Add your **machines** (e.g., CNC, 3D Printer) and **materials**
    - Connect your BOM and CAD models using your Onshape API key

3. **During Build Season**
    - Use the **Robot Dashboard** for overall robot progress
    - Use **System Dashboards** to manage subsystems (Intake, Elevator, etc.)
    - Students update statuses live as parts move through machining

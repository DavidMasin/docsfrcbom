## Troubleshooting

- **I can’t sign in**: Confirm your team number and password with your lead. Try a different browser or clear site data.
- **3D viewer is black/blank**: Your browser must support WebGL2; update Chrome/Edge/Firefox. Disable ad/script blockers for frcbom.com.
- **Missing parts in a system**: Ask your lead to re‑fetch the BOM from Onshape, or refresh after 2 minutes; the page auto-updates.
- **Downloads fail**: Ensure you’re signed in and your team has machine formats defined. Try again; large exports can take time.

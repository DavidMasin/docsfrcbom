title: First-Time Setup Checklist
---

Follow these steps in order:

1. **Create your team in FRCBOM** (admin + user credentials).
2. **Define Materials & Machines** in Team Settings.
3. **Create custom Properties in Onshape Team Settings** (see next page).
4. **Install the FeatureScript** in your Onshape document(s).
5. **Prepare your Onshape BOM** (columns & mapping).
6. **Connect Onshape to FRCBOM** (BOM URL, GLTF URLs, subassemblies).
7. **Verify dashboards**: Robot, Systems, and 3D Viewer.
8. **Invite team members** and assign roles.

- **Dashboard:** Large action buttons (CNC, 3D Printer, Lathe, Mill, Gerung, Show All Parts, Show In-House, Show COTS).
- **BOM Grid:** Live‑updating quantities, status colors, and filters (machine, material, COTS/In‑House).
- **Part Drawer:** Click a part for details, GLTF preview, and actions.
- **System & Robot Dashboards:** Progress stats, persistent 3D viewer, and confetti when parts are finished.

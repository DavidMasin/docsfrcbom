### Downloading CAD
- The **download** button picks the right format based on the **current process** (or the machine that finished the part).
- Filenames include **part name**, **quantity**, and **material** (e.g., `Gusset x4 - 5052-H32.dxf`).

> **Pro tip:** Use **Download All Material** to export everything needed for your next cut/print job.

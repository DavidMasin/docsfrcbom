### Suggested workflows

**Router day**
1. Filter by your **Router** machine.
2. Set **Material** to the stock you’re cutting now.
3. Click **Download All Material**.
4. After cutting, update counts for **Process 1**.

**COTS check**
1. Filter **COTS**.
2. Type quantities on the cards to track what’s in stock.
3. Green cards = you’re covered; white/yellow may need ordering or work.

**Subsystem sprint**
1. Open **System Dashboard** to see overall completion.
2. Work from **current process** labels on cards.
3. Watch the ring move—celebrate when parts “finish”! 🎉
